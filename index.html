Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client

Cisco AnyConnect client lets you to connect to your remote organization in a secured way. To start using AnyConnect you need to install it in your workstation first and you may need administrator/root priviledge for that.

Download

There are severl versions of AnyConnect. It's always a good idea to use the latest version available. Dependinig on what operating system you have in your workstation you need different software or packages.

  • AnyConnect Client for Windows
  • AnyConnect Client for MacOS
  • AnyConnect Client for Linux

Connecting to VPN

To connect to a remote VPN, you need at least two peice of information, a gateway address(IP or FQDN) and a valid credentials. Both of these will be provided by your network administrator. Usually a gateway address looks like this: https://example.url.org and credential is "yourname", "$omePassW0rd". Once you got the right gateway address and credentials, use that to connect to VPN.

After installing AnyConnect in your workstation, follow below steps.
  1. Copy the FQDN address and paste it in AnyConnect and click on "Connect".




  2. Enter your username and password, then click on "OK".
